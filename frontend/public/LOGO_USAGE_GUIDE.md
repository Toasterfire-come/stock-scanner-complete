## üé® Design Specifications

### Icon Design
**Concept:** Three ascending vertical bars representing growth and data analysis

**Dimensions:**
- **Standard:** 64x64px
- **Favicon:** 32x32px (optimized)
- **Scalable:** SVG format for all sizes

**Bar Heights (64px version):**
- **Short bar (left):** 16px height
- **Medium bar (center):** 24px height
- **Tall bar (right):** 36px height

**Bar Width:** 12px each
**Spacing:** 2px between bars
**Border Radius:** 1.2px (10% rounded corners)

**Visual Principle:** Subtle upward progression without looking like a retail chart

## üìê Usage Guidelines

### Minimum Sizes

**Horizontal Logo:**
- **Digital:** Minimum 120px wide
- **Print:** Minimum 1 inch (2.54cm) wide

**Vertical Logo:**
- **Digital:** Minimum 80px wide
- **Print:** Minimum 0.75 inch (1.9cm) wide

**Icon Only:**
- **Digital:** Minimum 24px √ó 24px
- **Print:** Minimum 0.25 inch (0.64cm)

### Clear Space
Maintain clear space around logo equal to the height of one bar (use medium bar as reference).

```

    [ LOGO ]
[ ---- CLEAR SPACE ---- ]
```

### Don'ts ‚ùå

- ‚ùå Do NOT add outlines/strokes

## üéØ Brand Consistency Tips

1. **Always use provided files** - Don't recreate or modify
2. **Maintain aspect ratios** - No stretching or squashing
3. **Use correct colors** - #0A0E17 or #3B82F6 only (+ white)
4. **Respect clear space** - Minimum one bar height around logo
5. **Test at actual size** - Verify legibility before publishing
6. **Keep it flat** - No gradients, shadows, or 3D effects
7. **Choose right variation** - Horizontal for wide spaces, vertical for narrow
8. **Ensure contrast** - Minimum 4.5:1 ratio with background
